The Glizzy Score™ — Methodology

No single list can capture the true glory of America's glizzy landscape. The Glizzy Score aggregates data across sources and weights multiple factors:

Formula

GlizzyScore = (spotCount × 12) + (avgSources × 8) + (stylePoints × 6) + (heritagePoints × 4) + (awardsPoints × 5)

Factor Breakdown

📍
Spot Density (×12)
How many nationally-recognized hot dog spots does the city have? More top spots = more glizzy culture.
📰
Source Citations (×8)
Average number of authoritative food publications (Eater, Thrillist, Food & Wine, Serious Eats, etc.) citing spots from this city.
🎨
Style Originality (×6)
Does the city have its OWN iconic hot dog style? Chicago, Detroit, and Tucson all get maximum points here.
🏛️
Heritage (×4)
How old is the oldest notable spot? Cities with century-old institutions earn more heritage points.
🏆
Awards (×5)
James Beard Awards, national landmark status, Travel Channel features, and other recognition.
